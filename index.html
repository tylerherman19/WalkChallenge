How It Works

Walk a little. Win a lot.

Register once, walk once a day on your Sperax pad, and log that session to earn your daily entry. It doesn't matter how many steps — one log per day, one entry per day, up to 15 total.

01

Register

Sign up below with your name, email, and Sperax product info. You'll receive your Participant ID by email immediately.

02

Use Your Pad

Walk on your Sperax pad once a day during the challenge — March 17 through March 31, 2026. One walk a day is all it takes.

03

Log Each Session

After your daily walk, visit the Step Submission page, enter your Participant ID and last name, and log your step count for that session. One log per day earns one entry — steps don't matter.

04

Winners Drawn

The competition closes March 31st. After entries close, three winners are selected by random draw. Walk every day March 17–31 and log each session for the maximum 15 entries.

Official Rules

Terms & Conditions

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. A purchase does not improve your chances of winning.

1. Eligibility

The Sperax Walk to Win Challenge ("Challenge") is open to legal residents of the United States who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry. Employees, officers, directors, agents, and representatives of Sperax and their immediate family members are not eligible to enter. Void where prohibited by law.

2. Challenge Period

The Challenge begins on March 17, 2026 at 12:00 AM Eastern Time ("ET") and ends on March 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET ("Challenge Period"). Registrations must be submitted by March 24, 2026. Step submissions are accepted through March 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET. Entries received outside this period will not be eligible.

3. How to Enter — No Purchase Necessary

To enter, complete the registration form on this page during the Challenge Period. You must provide your full legal name, valid email address, Sperax product model, and purchase retailer. No purchase is necessary to enter or win. Once registered, you may submit one (1) step log per calendar day via the Step Submission page at speraxchallenge.com/submit-steps.html. Each daily submission must include the date of the session and your step count. Any step count is valid — there is no minimum. Each daily submission constitutes one (1) separate draw entry, for a maximum of fifteen (15) entries total across the fifteen-day Challenge Period. Limit one (1) registration per person. Duplicate registrations will be disqualified.

4. Prize Description

  • Three (3) winners will each receive: (a) one (1) digital Visa Gift Card valued at $5,000 USD; and (b) a full rebate equal to the verified original purchase price of their Sperax product (maximum $499 USD).
  • Total approximate retail value (ARV) per prize package: up to $5,499 USD.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and may not be substituted or redeemed for cash except at Sperax's sole discretion.
  • All federal, state, and local taxes applicable to prizes are the sole responsibility of the winner.

5. Winner Selection & Notification

Three (3) winners will be selected by random draw from all eligible entries after the competition closes on March 31, 2026. Winners will be notified via email using the address provided at registration. Winners have forty-eight (48) hours from the time of notification to respond and confirm prize acceptance. Failure to respond within 48 hours may result in forfeiture and selection of an alternate winner. Sperax is not responsible for undelivered, misdirected, or delayed email notifications.

6. Prize Delivery

The digital Visa Gift Card will be emailed to the winner's registered email address within three (3) business days of winner confirmation. The purchase rebate will be applied to the original payment method used at time of Sperax purchase within five to seven (5–7) business days of confirmation. Winners must provide valid proof of purchase (order number or receipt) to claim the rebate portion of the prize.

7. General Conditions

  • Sperax reserves the right to cancel, suspend, or modify the Challenge if fraud, technical failures, or any other factor beyond its reasonable control impairs the integrity or proper functioning of the Challenge.
  • Sperax is not responsible for lost, late, incomplete, invalid, unintelligible, or misdirected registrations or step submissions.
  • By participating, entrants agree to be bound by these Official Rules and the final decisions of Sperax in all matters related to the Challenge.
  • By accepting a prize, winners consent to use of their name and general likeness for promotional purposes without additional compensation, where permitted by applicable law.
  • This Challenge is governed by the laws of the United States. Any disputes shall be resolved in accordance with applicable US law.
  • Entrants release Sperax and its affiliates from any and all liability related to participation in this Challenge or the acceptance or use of any prize.

8. Privacy

Personal information collected during this Challenge will be used solely for the administration of the Challenge and, with consent, for future Sperax communications. We will never sell or share your personal information with third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
